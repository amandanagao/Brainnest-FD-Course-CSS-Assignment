Meet our Chefs

  It all started with a small, but passionate group of chefs whom wanted to bring their cuisines to the metro area to the public at large. Before the opening of our wonderful restaurant, the local cuisine was typical American style. We all wanted to change this, we all wanted to bring in something new and exciting to the downtown metro area. Thus the idea was born and Yummy Yummm, was born.

  Our goal is to educate the public of unique tastes and succulent dishes from around the world. Each chef brought with them their culture and personal experiences enriching Yummy Yummm as it grew into a restaurant with a one-of-a-kind atmosphere that remains unrivaled! So come on in, relax, and prepare for an experience found nowhere else!
